How BMI is Calculated

BMI means Body Mass Index. It is calculated using weight and height to estimate whether you are underweight, normal, overweight or obese.

BMI Categories

  • Below 18.5 = Underweight
  • 18.5 to 24.9 = Normal Weight
  • 25 to 29.9 = Overweight
  • 30+ = Obesity
