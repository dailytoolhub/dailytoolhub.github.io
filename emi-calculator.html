What is EMI?

EMI means Equated Monthly Installment. It is the fixed monthly payment made to repay a loan over a chosen tenure.

This calculator is useful for home loans, personal loans, bike loans, education loans, and car finance.

Benefits of EMI Calculator

  • Compare banks and lenders
  • Plan your budget properly
  • Understand interest cost
  • Choose best repayment tenure
